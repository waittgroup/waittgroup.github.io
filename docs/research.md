# Research

_Last updated: 2026-07-18_

The Waitt Group works at the intersection of computational chemistry
and taste science, combining quantum-mechanical (QM) molecular
descriptors with machine learning to understand and predict taste.

## Research areas

### QM-derived molecular descriptors

Computing quantum-mechanical descriptors (e.g. via ORCA) for small
molecules relevant to taste perception, as inputs for downstream ML
models.

### ML taste prediction

Building machine learning models that predict taste properties
(sweetness, bitterness, etc.) from molecular structure and QM
descriptors.

### Computational workflows

Scaling QM calculations using containerized ORCA on OSPool/HTCondor —
see the [SOPs](sops/orca-ospool-workflow.md) section for the current
workflow.
